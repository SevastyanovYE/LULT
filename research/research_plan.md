# Research Plan

## Research Areas

- CEFR: official companion volume, descriptors, mediation, alignment cautions.
- ACTFL: proficiency guidelines and comparison limits.
- ALTE: testing quality, can-do statements, alignment practices.
- EALTA: ethical and fair language testing guidance.
- Cambridge English assessment principles: validity, reliability, impact, practicality.
- Adaptive testing, CAT, and IRT: item calibration, stopping rules, measurement error.
- Writing assessment: analytic rubrics, rater reliability, task effects.
- Speaking assessment: fluency, pronunciation, interaction, audio constraints.

## Source Types To Seek

- Official standards and manuals.
- Systematic reviews or meta-analyses where available.
- Peer-reviewed assessment research.
- Technical reports from reputable assessment organizations.
- University language testing research centers.

## Quality Criteria

Apply `source_quality_protocol.md`: evidence tier, venue quality, methods transparency, conflicts of interest, and clear uncertainty notes.
