## Open Response

Prompt S-A2  
Target level: A2  
Skill: speaking  
Prompt: Talk for 30 seconds about your daily routine.  
Scoring notes: simple present, sequence words, intelligibility if audio exists.  
Diagnoses: routine spoken production.

Prompt S-B1  
Target level: B1  
Skill: speaking  
Prompt: Talk for 60 seconds about a trip, event, or weekend you remember well.  
Scoring notes: past narrative, sequence, repair strategies.  
Diagnoses: connected speech on familiar topics.

Prompt S-B2  
Target level: B2  
Skill: speaking  
Prompt: Give your opinion: should companies allow remote work? Explain two reasons.  
Scoring notes: fluency, opinion support, discourse markers.  
Diagnoses: B2 spoken argument.

Prompt S-C1  
Target level: C1  
Skill: speaking  
Prompt: Discuss a trade-off between convenience and privacy.  
Scoring notes: abstraction, nuance, lexical precision, interaction.  
Diagnoses: advanced spoken flexibility.
