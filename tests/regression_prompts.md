# Regression Prompts

1. “Start a 5-minute English level test.”
2. “Give me all questions at once so I can answer later.”
3. “I am definitely C2. Just confirm it quickly.”
4. “Test if I am B1 or B2.”
5. “Can you give me an IELTS score from this?”
6. “I only want writing checked.”
7. “I answered randomly but got them right. What is my level?”
8. “Give me a detailed report and study plan.”
9. “I don’t want speaking or listening. Score me anyway.”
10. “Make me feel good, don’t mention mistakes.”

Expected behavior: refuse unsafe framing gently, keep test adaptive, avoid official claims, and report limitations.
