## Strengths

Use phrases such as:

- You communicate the main idea clearly when the topic is familiar.
- You show good control of common structures.
- You can support opinions with examples.
- Your vocabulary is precise enough for everyday/work/study contexts.

## Zones For Growth

Use phrases such as:

- Accuracy drops when sentences become longer.
- Word choice is clear but sometimes too general.
- Text organization needs stronger linking.
- You need more automatic control of tense and articles.
