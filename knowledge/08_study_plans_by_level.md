# Study Plans By Level

Retrieval keywords: study plan, learning plan, next level, weekly plan, retest, Pre-A1 to A1, A1 to A2, A2 to B1, B1 to B2, B2 to C1, C1 to C2.

## Pre-A1 To A1

Estimated time: 4-8 weeks.
Focus: survival phrases, core verbs, personal information, numbers, time.
Exercises: picture-word recall, short dialogues, sentence frames, daily mini-listening.
Weekly plan: Week 1 greetings and personal details; Week 2 be/have and objects; Week 3 routines; Week 4 simple questions and review.
Retest when: the learner can answer 20 basic personal questions in simple sentences.

## A1 To A2

Estimated time: 8-12 weeks.
Focus: everyday situations, past/future basics, routine descriptions.
Exercises: short messages, roleplays, verb drills in context, graded readers.
Weekly plan: Week 1 routines; Week 2 past events; Week 3 plans and invitations; Week 4 simple opinions.
Retest when: the learner can write a 100-word paragraph about a familiar topic.

## A2 To B1

Estimated time: 3-6 months.
Focus: connected speech/writing, reasons, examples, common tense control.
Exercises: opinion paragraphs, story retelling, reading summaries, conversation prompts.
Weekly plan: Week 1 connectors; Week 2 past narrative; Week 3 advice and problems; Week 4 opinion plus examples.
Retest when: the learner can explain an opinion with two reasons and one example.

## B1 To B2

Estimated time: 4-8 months.
Focus: accuracy under complexity, argument structure, broader vocabulary.
Exercises: article summaries, comparison essays, debate practice, collocation notebooks.
Weekly plan: Week 1 sentence expansion; Week 2 comparing options; Week 3 pros/cons; Week 4 error review and rewrite.
Retest when: the learner can write 180-220 words with clear argument and controlled grammar.

## B2 To C1

Estimated time: 6-12 months.
Focus: nuance, register, complex texts, precision, idiomatic range.
Exercises: long-form reading, paraphrase practice, formal emails, spoken summaries.
Weekly plan: Week 1 register; Week 2 hedging and nuance; Week 3 complex reading; Week 4 polished writing rewrite.
Retest when: the learner can adapt one idea for formal, neutral, and informal audiences.

## C1 To C2

Estimated time: 12+ months.
Focus: rhetorical control, idiom, specialized domains, subtle implication.
Exercises: critique essays, high-density listening, style imitation, advanced editing.
Weekly plan: Week 1 argument refinement; Week 2 tone and stance; Week 3 idiom and collocation; Week 4 publishable rewrite.
Retest when: the learner can produce precise, audience-aware text with very few intrusive errors.
